# MIRA homepage review prototype

Standalone, high-fidelity homepage concept for team review. This repository does not contain or modify production MIRA code.

## The one-sentence promise

Direct your TikTok feed without opening TikTok every day.

## What the opening story must communicate

1. **You write:** the author stays focused on the book.
2. **You direct:** bring existing assets, create what is missing in MIRA, or use Booketeer as the optional whole-manuscript path.
3. **Press play:** approve the content, accounts, delivery mode, and schedule before starting a Bucket.
4. **Readers discover:** MIRA publishes only approved combinations and reports discovery signals while the author keeps control.

## Product rules represented in the prototype

- A Bucket needs content and one or more TikTok accounts.
- Content means the visual, on-screen text, caption, hashtags, and presentation style.
- Authors may bring every asset, create images and slide decks in MIRA, or mix both paths.
- Booketeer is optional. It maps a whole manuscript into structured book intelligence; an indexed book is not required to use MIRA.
- Authors may connect accounts they already own. Buying or renting an account is not required.
- Direct Post and TikTok Draft are both represented.
- Generated content, Booketeer, and managed accounts are separate optional costs.
- Views, saves, follows, and book clicks are discovery signals. Sales and rank are possible downstream outcomes, not promises.

## Review checklist

- Can a first-time author explain MIRA after the first four scroll beats?
- Is the difference between Booketeer and MIRA obvious?
- Is bring-your-own as prominent as generation?
- Does the pre-launch confirmation make the author feel in control?
- Does the page avoid implying guaranteed sales or rank?
- Is the language comfortable for an author who does not enjoy TikTok or technical setup?

## Prototype interactions

- Scroll through the four-stage opening cinematic.
- Use the stage rail on desktop to jump between Write, Direct, Run, and Discover.
- Switch between “I already have content” and “I need content.”
- Change Bucket cadence and pause or resume the campaign.
- Switch between Direct Post and TikTok Draft.
- Open Sign in and enter any email other than `member@mira.test` to review the new-author recovery flow.

## Validation performed

- Desktop: 1280 × 720.
- Mobile: 390 × 844 with no horizontal overflow.
- Reduced motion: all four explanations remain readable and the decorative world is removed.
- Sign-in recovery preserves the entered email and explicitly states that no sign-in email was sent.
- Interactive content-path and Bucket controls update correctly.
- Runtime error counter remained at zero throughout the checks.
